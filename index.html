JEREMY T. CARLOT

ABOUT ME

I was born on the French west coast. Since my childhood, I have dedicated a great place in my heart to the ocean. Both swimming and beach lover, I used to spend every free time either in a swimming pool for practicing or on the beach for surfing, contemplating waves rolling, or observing littoral species. Enjoying traveling, I’ve visited reef islands ecosystems during my bachelor and sadly found how these ecosystems are vulnerable to climate change. In this context, I decided to start a Ph.D. on coastal protection in French Polynesia.
